Menemen

Preparation

  1. Wash the tomatoes.
  2. Dry them.
  3. Cut the pappers.
  4. Put the olive oil in to the pan.
  5. Heat the oil.
  6. Put the papper in to the pan.
  7. Fry them for 5 minutes.

    8. by the way,

  8. Peel the tomatoes.
  9. Chop them.
  10. Add the tomato to the pepper.
  11. Sprinkle the salt and pepper onto the mixture.
  12. Mix them.
  13. Cook for minutes.
  14. Beat the eggs in the bowl.
  15. Pour the egg onto the mixture.
  16. Cook for 1 minute.

    17. The menemen is ready. Serve it hot. Enjoy!

Ingredients (For 2 Persons)

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 green peppers
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper
  • 3 tablespons of olive oil

Utensils

  • 1 pan
  • 1 chopping board
  • 1 knife
  • 1 wooden spoon
  • 1 bowl
